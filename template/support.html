Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with the app? +

Getting started is easy! Simply download the app from the App Store, create an account using your email, and follow the onboarding tutorial. You'll be guided through all the main features step by step.

Is my data secure and private? +

Absolutely. We take your privacy seriously. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We do not share your personal information with third parties. Please read our Privacy Policy for more details.

How do I cancel my subscription? +

You can cancel your subscription anytime through your device's App Store settings. Go to Settings > Your Name > Subscriptions, select our app, and tap "Cancel Subscription". Your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period.

Why is the app not working properly? +

Try these basic troubleshooting steps: 1) Make sure you have the latest version installed, 2) Restart the app, 3) Check your internet connection, 4) Restart your device. If the problem persists, please contact our support team with details about the issue.

How can I restore my previous purchase? +

If you've purchased a subscription or made an in-app purchase, you can restore it by going to Settings in the app and tapping "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.

Do you offer a free trial? +

Yes! We offer a free trial period for new users. The trial duration varies by subscription plan. You can cancel anytime during the trial period without being charged.
