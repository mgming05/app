Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

Runlore is a personal running journal designed to be private by default. This policy explains what the app does — and does not do — with your data.

No account, no servers

Runlore has no user accounts and no backend of its own. Your runs, routes, and diary entries are stored only on this device. We do not collect, transmit, or sell your personal data.

Location

With your permission, the app uses GPS — including in the background while a run is in progress — to record your route, distance, pace, and elevation. Location is used only to track your run and is saved on this device. It is never uploaded to us.

Microphone & speech recognition

When you record a voice diary, the app accesses the microphone and uses Apple's Speech framework to transcribe your words to text. Transcription is performed on-device wherever your device supports it. The audio recording is used only for transcription and is deleted afterward — it is not kept. Only the edited text you choose to save is stored.

Body weight

If you enter your weight, it is stored on this device and used only to estimate calories burned. It is not shared.

No tracking or ads

Runlore contains no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking SDKs.

Your control

Because all data lives on this device, deleting an entry removes it, and deleting the app removes everything associated with it. You may also revoke location, microphone, or speech permissions at any time in the system Settings.

Children

Runlore is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to the developer at runlore@gmail.com, or through the App Store listing.

隐私政策

最近更新:2026 年 6 月 22 日

Runlore 是一款以隐私为先的个人跑步日记。本政策说明 App 会如何处理(以及不会如何处理)你的数据。

无账号、无服务器

Runlore 没有用户账号,也没有自有后端。你的跑步记录、轨迹和日记仅保存在本机。我们不收集、不上传、不出售你的个人数据。

位置信息

在你授权后,App 会使用 GPS(包括跑步进行中切到后台时)记录你的路线、距离、配速和爬升。位置仅用于记录本次跑步并保存在本机,绝不会上传给我们。

麦克风与语音识别

录制语音日记时,App 会访问麦克风,并使用 Apple 的语音框架把语音转写为文字。在设备支持的情况下转写在本机完成。录音仅用于转写,转写后即删除、不保留;只有你选择保存的编辑后文字才会被存储。

体重

如果你填写体重,它仅保存在本机,且只用于估算消耗的卡路里,不会被分享。

无追踪、无广告

Runlore 不含任何统计分析、广告或第三方追踪 SDK。

你的掌控权

由于所有数据都在本机,删除某条记录即从设备移除,卸载 App 即清除全部相关数据。你也可以随时在系统「设置」中收回位置、麦克风或语音识别权限。

儿童

Runlore 不面向 13 岁以下儿童,也不会有意收集其数据。

联系我们

如对本政策有疑问,可发送邮件至 runlore@gmail.com,或通过 App Store 页面联系开发者。
